ABOUT Virat Kohli

Details...

Full Name: Virat Kohli
Date of Birth: November 5, 1988
Place of Birth: Delhi, India
Role: Batsman (Right-handed)
Teams: India, Royal Challengers Bangalore (in the Indian Premier League - IPL)

Achievements of Virat Kohli..

  • Captaincy: Virat Kohli has been the captain of the Indian national cricket team in all three formats - Test, One Day International (ODI), and Twenty20 International (T20I).

  • Batting Records: Kohli is considered one of the best batsmen in the world and has numerous records to his name. He has consistently been among the top run-scorers in all formats of the game.

  • and T20I Captaincy: Under Kohli's captaincy, India achieved significant success in ODIs and T20Is. India became the number one ranked team in ODIs during his captaincy tenure.

  • Test Cricket: Kohli led India to historic Test series victories, including the first-ever Test series win in Australia during the 2018-2019 season.

  • ICC Awards: Virat Kohli has received several prestigious awards from the International Cricket Council (ICC), including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year on multiple occasions.
