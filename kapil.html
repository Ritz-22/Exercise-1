Achievements of Kapil Dev..

  • 1983 Cricket World Cup: Kapil Dev led the Indian cricket team to its first-ever Cricket World Cup victory in 1983. India defeated the West Indies in the final at Lord's, and Kapil's inspirational knock of 175 against Zimbabwe in the group stage is considered one of the greatest innings in World Cup history.

  • All-Round Prowess: Kapil Dev was known for his exceptional skills both as a batsman and a fast-medium bowler. He scored over 5,000 runs in Test cricket and took more than 400 wickets, making him one of the leading all-rounders in the game.

  • Test Cricket: Kapil Dev played 131 Test matches for India, scoring over 5,000 runs at an average of around 31. He took 434 wickets in Test cricket, making him India's highest wicket-taker in Tests at that time.

  • Padma Bhushan Award: Kapil Dev was honored with the Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian award, in 1991.
